UnitPrice Analysis

  1. This "unique gift" supplier's money maker was overwhelmingly cheap items, with 92.3% of all items sold costing less than $5.00, accounting for 71.4% of total sales.
  2. Medium priced items accounted for 7.7% of all items sold, and 27% of total sales.
  3. High priced items accounted for less than 0.1% of all items sold, and 0.7% of total saless.

Sales Frequency Analysis

  1. "Regular" selling items made 39.4% of total sales for this wholeseller. Items that include a design in the description are far and away the best selling created category

  3. best seller: 24.8% of total sales
  4. hot: 17.3% of total sales
  5. regular seller: 39.4% of total sales

Main Takeaways

  1. "Regular" selling items made 39.4% of total sales for this wholeseller.
  2. Items that include a design in the description are far and away the best selling created category. Second are items that include some indication of being for household(for person not business) in the description, and Third items that include color in description.
  3. This Gift Shop's money maker by Unit Price was overwhelmingly cheap items, with 92.3% of all items sold costing less than $5.00, accounting for 71.4% of total sales.
